Your Privacy
// Exactly what happens to your data
🔒
Nothing is stored. Ever.

LoanLens has no database. We don't log, save, or sell anything you submit. Your loan data is processed in memory and immediately discarded.

📄
Uploaded documents: Claude AI reads them

When you upload a document, the image is sent to Claude AI to extract the text. It is not stored anywhere after that. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that processing.

✍️
Manual entry: only numbers, nothing personal

If you type in your loan terms, only those numbers are sent for analysis. No name, no address, no account numbers — just the loan math.

🚫
We do not sell data. We cannot — we don't have any.

Our backend is a stateless proxy. It receives your request, calls Claude, and returns the result. Nothing is written to disk.
